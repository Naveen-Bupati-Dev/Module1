What is dairy and why we enjoyit!

Dairy is a type of food that comes from animals like cows, goats, sheep, and buffalo. It includes items like milk, cheese, yogurt, and butter. We enjoy dairy because it is a great source of protein, calcium, and other vitamins and minerals. It can be used in a variety of ways, from making smoothies to baking cakes. It also adds flavor to dishes and can be used as a topping or a dip. Dairy is a versatile food that can be used in many ways, making it a popular choice for people of all ages.
